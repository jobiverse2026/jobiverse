# JobiVerse Launch Checklist

## Resume Studio monetization — required before launch

- Finalize prices for individual CV templates and service bundles.
- Decide whether preview/edit is free and PDF export requires purchase.
- Connect payment gateway and verify successful, failed and refunded payments.
- Add template purchase records and candidate entitlement checks.
- Lock paid PDF exports and premium template access server-side.
- Add invoices, GST treatment, refund terms and support workflow.
- Clearly display prices before checkout.
